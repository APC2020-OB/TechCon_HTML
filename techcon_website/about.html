History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small meetup for tech enthusiasts. Over the years, it has grown into one of the largest technology conferences in Africa, hosting thousands of developers, innovators, and leaders from across the globe.

Mission

Our mission is to empower innovation, connect minds, and shape the future of technology by providing a platform for learning, networking, and showcasing cutting-edge advancements.

Past Speakers

TechCon has hosted some of the most influential voices in tech:

  • Ada Lovelace

    Pioneering computer scientist known for her work on the Analytical Engine.

  • Tim Berners-Lee

    Inventor of the World Wide Web, advocating for open web standards.

  • Grace Hopper

    Computer programming pioneer and Navy rear admiral who helped develop COBOL.
